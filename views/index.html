Roomba® 614 Features

Two brushes that totally get teamwork.

Dual Multi-Surface Brushes work together to grab dust, dirt, and large debris to help thoroughly clean your floors. The first brush loosens, lifts, and pulls in dust, dirt, and hair from floors and in carpet, the second accelerates debris into the suction channel.

Dirt and dust can’t hide.

The 3-Stage Cleaning System packs a serious punch when it comes to messes. With Dual Multi-Surface Brushes and a specially designed Edge-Sweeping Brush, no crumb is safe.

Needs no time adjusting.

The Auto-Adjust Cleaning Head adapts its height to keep the Dual Multi-Surface Brushes in close contact with different floor surfaces, directly engaging with both hard floors and carpets.

Senses and eliminates dirt.

Dirt Detect sensors alert your robot about dirtier areas of your home, then clean them more thoroughly, so high-traffic spots stay as clean as the rest of the house.

Take cleaning to the edge.

The Edge-Sweeping Brush is specially designed at a 27-degree angle to sweep debris away from edges and corners.
